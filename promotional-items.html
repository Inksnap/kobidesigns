Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Most items start at 50-100 units, but some premium products may have lower minimums.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 10-14 business days after artwork approval. Rush options are available for many items.

Can I see a sample before ordering?

Yes, we offer virtual proofs for all orders. Physical samples are available for most items for a nominal fee.

What file formats do you accept for logos?

We prefer vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) for best results. High-resolution JPG or PNG files may work for some applications.
