Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For most apparel items, we require a minimum of 10 pieces per design.

What file formats do you accept for designs?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. Vector formats are preferred for best results.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 7-10 business days after design approval. Rush options are available for an additional fee.

Can I get samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we offer sample production for a small fee. This allows you to check the quality and fit before committing to a large order.
